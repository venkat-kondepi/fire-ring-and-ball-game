Welcome to the Ball Game!

Game Instructions

  • Press the Arrow Up key or Jump button to make the ball jump.
  • Pass through the fire ring to score points.
  • Avoid missing the fire ring, or you will lose a life.
  • You have 3 lives. If you miss the fire ring 3 times, the game will end.
  • Your score increases each time you pass through the ring.
